01
Attorney / Lawyer
A licensed professional who advises and represents clients in legal matters. Requires a J.D. degree and passing the bar exam. Strong demand in civil rights, corporate, and tech law. Average salary: $120K+. Matches my skills in critical thinking, persuasive writing, and public speaking.
02
AI / Software Developer
Creates intelligent programs and systems using machine learning and coding. One of the fastest growing fields globally. Average salary: $100K to $180K. I am already building projects in this area independently in my free time.
03
Legal Tech Specialist
A hybrid career combining law and technology, helping law firms use AI, software, and data tools. Growing rapidly as AI transforms the legal industry. Average salary: $90K to $140K. The perfect career that combines both of my passions.
